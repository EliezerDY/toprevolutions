Revolutions

Revolutions have occured many times throughout history. In this site we will examin four of the major revolutions of the modern era.

  1. The Dutch Revolution which brought the first republic to Europe.
  2. The French Revoltion and subsequent Napolianic wars which spread the ideas of Enlightment through Europe.
  3. The American Revolution which led to the Unites States of America developing into a superpower in modern day history.
  4. The Russian Revolution which put an end to the reign of the Tsars and led to the world's first communist run country.
